Who stole my energy?

Posted on January 11, 2017 by kirstinchaplin

I’ll be honest. I thought that, by now, almost two weeks into this no drinking malarky I would be feeling well, better than I do.

This morning, when the alarm went off at 6.15am (yes, that is early isn’t it? And to think I was contemplating getting up even earlier to do some yoga! Pah) I felt as though I had been hit with a sledge hammer.

I’ve also deduced that alcohol might not be the only cause of my crazy mood swings because they are 100% still happening. Yesterday I swung from irrationally happy that I have a tumble dryer and that it dried our clothes (I mean ) to total overwhelm about cooking / fashion / work / interior design. This stuff seems so easy to everyone else but, every day, it threatens to swollow me up.

Anyway, I digress. My sugar consumption has also gone through the ROOF. I literally can’t shove enough of the stuff into my face and, again, it feels too overwhelming to tackle this as well as give up booze / cope with life / put makeup on in the mornings!

Perhaps this crazy phase of adjustment will pass soon. I hope so.

Meanwhile, all around me, people are falling off the Dry January wagon faster than you can say ‘herbal tea’ which makes me a teeny bit envious that they will soon be locking lips with a delicious fruity red.

OK, enough rambling for one day. Time to push on with the day.

K x

P.S. My Saturday headache turned into a full-blown migraine so, in the interests of fairness, I doubt very much it was caused by the Becks Blue.
